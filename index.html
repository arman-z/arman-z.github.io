About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Meta AI (FAIR), working with Yuandong Tian on the intersection of machine learning and optimization, recently involving AI-guided optimization, reinforcement learning and combinatorial optimization. Prior to that, I earned my Ph.D. degree at University of California, Merced (UCM) advised by Miguel Á. Carreira-Perpiñán, where I primarily studied learning algorithms for decision trees and tree-based models (see TAO).
